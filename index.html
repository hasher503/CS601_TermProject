Hi,
I'm Hope.

I'm a graduate student studying Software Development at Boston University. I live in Richmond, Virginia, but I love my hometown of Portland, Oregon. My previous career was as a staff director at a residential treatment center for young people who struggle with addiction and mental health challenges. I decided to pursue software development after taking CS50 and discovering that I love making processes more efficient and solving problems. I'm still passionate about mental health treatment and destigmatization, and one day I'd love to write software for a company that uses technology to help people lead fulfilling lives.
